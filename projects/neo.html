Neo the Comfort Cat
Interactive Creative Project
For my DIG 5487 Creative Project, I crocheted cat out of african flower patches with sound reactive lights installed inside and capacitive touch patches. This project is meant to represent the feeling of anxiety, being represented by the lights, while having something or someone to calm you down, being represented by the cat. The lights respond to the noise level in the environment. In its calm state, blue lights slowly pulse. As the room gets louder, the lights will change to red, through a purple transition and the pulsing becomes faster. When the lights reach red, it is at its most panicked state. When a person pets the cat at one of its touch sensors, the lights will slowly return to its calm state. Most of the cat's body is made out of off-white and tie-dye acrylic yarn. The patches that serve as touch sensors are crocheted fully with the tie-dye yarn and conductive thread. Additionally, I used the motion sparkle mini and neopixel lights for the interaction and output.
